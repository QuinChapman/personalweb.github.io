Project Background


Trayminder is a mobile app that helps users keep track of information regarding their clear aligner (i.e. Invisalign, ClearCorrect, etc.) journey. The goal is to assist users in getting the best possible results from their treatment by providing features such as wear time, calendar notifications,and documentation of their smile transformation.

This app has made a profound difference in my Invisalign journey thus far, and while it has simplified the tracking process for many, like myself, the overall user experience can be greatly improved. In my redesign, I sought to improve the discoverability of options, information architecture,
design consistency (for iOS users), and overall visual design.
