Project Background


The Bellabeat data analysis case study was one of three case study options in the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate program. During this case study, I was required to to answer the key business questions while following the steps of the data analysis process: ask, prepare, process, analyze, share, and act.


R was used to clean, transform, and analyze the dataset provided by FitBit Fitness Tracker Data.


The business task of this study required analyzing smart device usage data in order to gain insight into how consumers use non-Bellabeat smart devices. The final deliverable included high-level insights and recommendations to the Bellabeat marketing analytics team.


Ultimately, this analysis sought to answer the following questions:

  • What are some trends in smart device usage?
  • How could these trends apply to Bellabeat customers?
  • How could these trends help influence Bellabeat marketing strategy?
