Project Background


For a semester-long project in my Web Application Development class, I was tasked with finding a client and creating an interactive website based on their needs.


My client was in search of a personal website to showcase his professional achievements. Ultimately, the goal was to allow the audience to connect with my client and showcase his personality and interests unlike what a typical resume would accomplish.


While expanding and refining my project based on peer critiques, I was required to incorporate HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery-based interactive elements and accesses to external data.
